GeLID

What is GeLID?

GeLID is a refrigerator on rent, a subscription that allows people to store their products and packages for cooling for hours, days and months.

Why this Project?

My own experience inspired me to begin this project. While I was in college, I lived in a studio apartment without a refrigerator, and while my girlfriend was visiting I cooked a dish for her, but her fight was rescheduled and the dish would go bad if I didn't cool it. In that moment, I wished there was a cooling service, so I could cool my stuff.
